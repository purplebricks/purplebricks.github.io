Mission

Make our customers deliriously happy when using our technology whilst continuously improving.

Vision

Become the biggest Estate Agency in the UK (not just the biggest online estate agency). At the same time become recognised as a major technology brand. Ultimately increase value for our shareholders.

Values

  • We respect, learn, create and innovate together.
  • We embrace change as part of everyday life.
  • We listen to input from all levels in the team.
  • We are flexible and independent, we make mistakes together and learn from them.
  • We have the freedom to innovate.
  • We care about the customer.
  • We are not micromanaged, we are all trusted and we all trust who we work with.
  • We can all can make a difference.
